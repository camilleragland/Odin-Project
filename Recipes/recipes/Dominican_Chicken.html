Dominican Chicken Recipe

Description:

This recipe is something I've learned from watching my mom cook chicken. It's a staple in my household, and it's usually how I prepare my chicken.

Ingredients

  • Thawed Chicken Breasts
  • Sazon Goya
  • Dried Parsely
  • Garlic Olive Oil
  • Adobo Chicken Seasoning
  • Black pepper
  • Garlic Powder
  • Onion Powder
  • Slap Ya Mama Seasoning
  • Oregano
  • Pure Lemon Juice
  • Chicken Bouillon

Steps

  1. Put thawed out chicken on a clean plate or cutting board
  2. Tenderize the chicken wth a tenderizer or just punch you feelings out
  3. Add Sazon goya massage it into the chicken
  4. Do the same with the parsely, adobo, black pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, slap ya mama seasoning, and oregano
  5. Put a pot or pan on a stove and have the stove been on setting 4
  6. Cut the chicken into strips, chucks, or however you want it
  7. Pour a tablespoon of carlic olive oil and a tablespoon of lemon juice in the pot/pan
  8. Put the chicken in the pot and put 2 tablespoons of chicken bouillon in the pot/pan
  9. Put a lid over the pot/pan to let it cook
  10. While cooking stir the chicken around every 5 minutes
  11. To check if the chicken is cooked, after 5-7 minutes grab a piece and slice to check if it's pink
  12. Once done put it in a containe, don't drain the juices!
  13. Serve with rice an vegetables, or ramen. Add the juices, it's delicious!
