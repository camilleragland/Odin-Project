Chocolate Cake Recipe

Description:

This recipe is what I use to make all boxed chocolate cake mix taste better. It's not exact measurements and it's just based on taste.

Ingredients

  • Chocolate Cake Mix
  • Milk - Any Kind
  • Eggs
  • Olive Oil
  • Cocoa Powder
  • Vanilla
  • Salt
  • Baking Soda
  • Powdered Sugar

Steps

  1. Grab a bowl and pour the chocolate cake mix into it
  2. Add 1 1/2 of any kind of milk into the bowl
  3. Crack and add 4 eggs into the mix
  4. Add 1 tablespoon of vanilla
  5. Add a 1 teaspoon of salt
  6. Add 1/3 cup of powdered sugar
  7. Add 1/4 cup of baking soda
  8. Add 1/3 cup of cocoa powder
  9. Preheat Oven to 345 degrees
  10. Mix the cake mix while the oven is preheating
  11. Butter the cake pan before pouring the mixture in
  12. Put the cake into the oven for 25-40 minutes
  13. To make sure the cake is done stick a tootpick/fork in it. If it comes out clean without cake ix it's done
  14. Let the cake completely coolr before puting it on a plae
  15. Voila! You have a cake!
