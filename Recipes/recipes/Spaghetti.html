Spaghetti Recipe

Description:

This recipe is just something I make when I'm craving spaghetti. It isn't a family recipe, and it's honestly just going by taste. So take this recipe with a grain of salt. There won't be exact measurements, so just taste it and smell it, and experiment. Enjoy your food, and experience the relaxation of cooking.

Ingredients

  • Dried Spaghetti Noodles
  • Prego Tomato Sauce
  • Fresh Mozzarella
  • Garlic Olive Oil
  • Thawed Ground Beef
  • Smoke Seasoning
  • Garlic Powder
  • Onion Powder
  • Slap Ya Mama Seasoning
  • Parsley
  • Oregano
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Bring a pot of water to boil
  2. Add salt and garlic olive oil to the boiling water
  3. Add the dried spaghetti noodles to the boiling pot of water
  4. Stir every 3 minutes of the noodles
  5. Take noodles out when it's cooked to not being to hot or dry
  6. Drain the noodles with a pasta strainer
  7. In a seperate pot put a tablespoon on Garlic Olive Oil
  8. Heat the pot on the setting of 4 and put thawed ground beef in the pot
  9. While cooking the ground beef chop it in the pan and season it
  10. Season the ground beef with garlic and onion powder, Slap Ya Mama seasoning, parsely, oregano, and smoke seasoning
  11. Cook the ground beaf until brown
  12. Drain the pot of ground beef juice
  13. Pour Prego Tomato Sauce in the pot, and mix the meat and sauce
  14. Season the meat sauce with garlic and onion powder, parsely, oregano, and Slap Ya Mama seasoning
  15. Put the sauce in a seperate container
  16. Serve the Spaghetti with or without sauce, preferably with it
